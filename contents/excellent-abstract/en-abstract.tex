This template targets the Jiangsu Ocean University school-level excellent thesis abstract submission. It separates stable thesis metadata from the abstract-specific writing sections so that the candidate can reuse the official title, author, supervisor, college, and program information while rewriting the Chinese abstract, the two-column body, and the English abstract as a compact standalone submission. The resulting workflow is more suitable for the official requirement that the abstract should present the same core information as the thesis in a concise and self-contained short paper.

Compared with the main thesis template, the excellent abstract emphasizes compression, evidence, and readability in a narrow page budget. The layout therefore keeps a full-width title and Chinese abstract block on the first page, then switches directly into a two-column body for background, method, results, and conclusion. This keeps the submission efficient to edit while preserving figures, references, and author bio elements required by the official format notes.
